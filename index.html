Our Programs

Aqua Babies

Our Parent and Tots program is designed with a ratio of 6:1 (Vaughan) and 7:1 (Barrie) to enhance the swimming experience for both guardians and tots. This program is a great starting point for children who still require their guardians with them in the water and any children who have minor swimming abilities. As the starting point of any swimming journey, this program gives your child the confidence and the basic skills required for swimming. These skills include, but are not limited to floating, gliding, kicking, jumping, rhythmic breathing and shallow water entries and exits.
